Zonta Club of Murnah

Under the gracious facilitation of Mr. Albert Owusu (Mr. Kofi), our benefactors, the Zonta Club of Murnah, Germany, provided grants for the upgrading our training and meeting centre at Atwima Boko. This gave the facility a facelift from the deteriorating structure it was to a more robust container that can accommodate more apprentices. We are grateful to the Zonta Club for this benevolent gesture.
