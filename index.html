GENTLE SOULS FOUNDATION - GSF

GSF is a non-governmental organization located at Atwima Boko in the Ashanti Region of Ghana focused at developing women and adolescent girls. Registered with the Registrar General’s Department of Ghana in December 2020, we aim to empower women and girls in the area of education and to inculcate in them the spirit of entrepreneurship and self-help initiatives to help improve their living conditions and reduce poverty. Due the economic hardships and general low living conditions women and especially girls usually face in the Atwima Boko environment, most of them resort to undignified lifestyles (especially sexual relationship with men) that ends up worsening their conditions, if not ruining their entire lives. In the Atwima Boko environment, several girls live in deplorable conditions that affect their education and general wellbeing. Low-income families struggle to provide adequately for the basic needs of girls, heightening period poverty, school dropouts and unwanted pregnancies and their rippling effects. It is in view of this that GSF was established to provide material, emotional and educational support systems to women and girls in low living conditions to improve their situation. Also, at GSF, we discuss pertinent issues concerning women such a menstruation and its associated problems, breast cancer and general health and wellbeing for women. Other areas of our work are single parenting, teenage pregnancies, livelihood for women, among others. Currently, we have 70 registered members from whom 20 are orphans living with their grandparents. Another 30 of them are products of teenage pregnancies who they are living with their single parents. Again, 10 are needy but brilliant students whom GSF has put under a scholarship scheme through Child Advocacy Foundation. For the remaining 10, 5 of them are from families with financial difficulties and 5 are teenage mothers who are under apprenticeship in dressmaking, soapmaking, beadmaking, etc. under the funding of GSF.
